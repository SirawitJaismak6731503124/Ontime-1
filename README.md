# Ontime

Ontime is a minimal Android app for building daily habits through time-based reminders.

No sign-up. No cloud. Just set a time, pick your days, and get notified — every week, on repeat.

## What it does

Create routines like "Morning run at 07:00" or "Take vitamins at 08:30" and Ontime will remind you at exactly the right time on the days you choose. Everything is stored locally on your device.
